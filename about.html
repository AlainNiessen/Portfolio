Let's introduce myself

Every person has their own unique story. Here is a glimpse into mine.

After a long career in the social sector as social worker, I decided in 2019 to study Webdevelopper and to follow my passion to Web technologies. During this phase, I have begun to incorporate different technologies to create responsive, user-friendly and high performance Websites.

I enjoy taking complex challenges, experimenting with different technologies to solve problems or to level-up the workflow and turning this work to simple and beauty Websites. I also love the logic and structure of coding and always strive to write elegant and efficient code.

When I'm not coding, you will find me walking around in nature or shooting pics. Yes, photography is my second big passion.
